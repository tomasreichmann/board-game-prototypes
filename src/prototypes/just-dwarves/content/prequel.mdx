# Prequel

You can play a quick standalone prequel game before the actual JUST DWARVES game. You can
get bonus gold based on your rank in the prequel. Also the winner of the prequel becomes the
starting player of the main game.

## Limitations

-   Card components only.
-   Maximum game-play time of 30 min.
-   It needs to be thematically connected to the main game.

## Components

### Cards

-   Bridge: 56x88mm
-   Thickness: 280-330gsm, eg. 0.23mm for 300gsm
-   36cards = 8.28mm total thickness
