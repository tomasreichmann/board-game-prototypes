_You jumped to the coordinates of the distress call and see a merchant transport entering a nearby asteroid field followed by a pirate corvette. Both ships seem pretty beat up. The transport's weapons and comms seem to be offline. They are trying to charge their Spin drive to jump away, but the disruptor fire from the pirate ship keeps draining its energy._

-   The transport is damaged and trying to escape through the asteroid field. Their comms seem to be offline.
-   If you want to engage, you will have to move to the asteroid field. Navigating it requires a skilled pilot, you risk damaging the ship, but you can also shield yourself from enemy fire.
-   The pirates seem to be ignoring you for now.
