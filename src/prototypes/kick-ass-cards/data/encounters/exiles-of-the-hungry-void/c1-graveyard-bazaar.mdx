## Buy upgrades for your ship

-   Each improvement costs Resources times next level. (E.g. Upgrade lasers from level 1 to level 2 costs 2 resources).
-   Systems available to install:
    -   Med bay: Reduces Injuries by 1 + Effect.
    -   Ritual room: +1 Effect on rituals.
    -   Teleport room: Can teleport crew in the room to a room in a nearby ship which does not have active shields.

## Buy and sell equipment
