# Missile Stockpile

-   üí° This encounter is a quick one-round threat ‚Üí resolution encounter. A missile battery launches 2x player count of different rockets immediatelly when the space-ship jumps in. Players can each try to destroy/hack/evade the rockets. Each missile that is not dealt with strikes a random system on the ship. The types of rockets are Incendiary, Breach, EMP, Toxic.

_The stars wrench themselves back into familiar points of light as you exit the jump. Before the ship even settles, proximity alarms scream to life. A nearby asteroid, disguised as inert rock, slides open to reveal an automated missile battery. It fires a volley before you can even raise shields._

**"Incoming! Multiple warheads!"**

_A number of different missiles (equal to **twice the number of crew members**) streaks toward your ship. They are a mixed bag of different technology and payloads different payload, a different flavor of disaster._

-   **Incendiary Missile:** Upon impact, starts a raging **Fire** in a random room it struck. Anyone in that room suffers **1 Burning** every turn until repaired.
-   **Breach Missile:** Causes a hull breach and vents the air from the room. Anyone in that room suffers **1 Freezing** every turn until repaired.
-   **EMP Missile:** Disables a random system, making it **Inoperable** for a round making the energy wasted.
-   **Toxic Missile:** Detonates a chemical payload, filling a random room with toxic gas. Anyone in that room takes **1 Distress** every turn until vented.

---

### Brace for Impact!

_You have seconds to act. Each crew member can try to intercept some missiles._

üé≠ **Choose your target and your method!**

For each crew member, pick one incoming missile to deal with.

-   **Destroy it:** Target a missile with the ship's point-defense cannons. On a success, it's vaporized in a flash of light. On a failure, your shot goes wide.
-   **Hack it:** Splice into the missile's guidance system, turning it into a dud or making it veer off course. On a success, the missile drifts harmlessly into space. On a failure, the counter-intrusion locks you out.
-   **Jam it:** Use the ship's electronic countermeasures to blind the missile's sensors. On a success, the missile loses its lock. On a failure, it homes in with deadly precision.

---

### Resolution

_The next few seconds are a blur of frantic activity, explosions, and near misses._

-   For each **Effect**, one missile is neutralized.
-   The rest of the missiles strike your ship.
    -   üí° For each missile that hits, roll a D4 to find out what type it is. Then roll a die to determine which system is struck. Apply the missile's effect._

_As quickly as it began, the assault is over. the echoes of explosions still ringing in your ears and the smell of ozone and smoke filling the corridors._

-   The automated battery, having expended its loaded missiles, retracts back into the asteroid.
-   You find a door on the other side of the asteroid, where a supply of missile parts are stored. There is a data-shard with missile schematics, but the machine spirit seems to only want to part with a single missile before it destroys the data-shard.
