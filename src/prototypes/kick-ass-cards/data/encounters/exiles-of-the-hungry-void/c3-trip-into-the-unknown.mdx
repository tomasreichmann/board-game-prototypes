### Into the Maelstrom

_The nebula is a swirling chaos of gas, dust, and gravitational anomalies. Each foray deeper into its core offers greater rewards but also exposes your ship to increasing danger. The ship's machine spirit plots a course, but the final decision is yours._

<CounterCard {...counterMap.investigation} title="Nebula Depth" current={0} total={2} />

üé≠ **Do you wish to proceed deeper?**

Each time you decide to go deeper, the **Nebula Depth** increases by 1. You must then make a **Piloting** or **Engineering** check to navigate the hazards. The difficulty of this check is equal to the current **Nebula Depth**.

-   **Success:** You navigate the treacherous currents and uncover something of value.
-   **Failure:** Your ship is battered by the nebula. It takes **1 Damage** to a random system for each point of **Effect** you failed the check by.

After each check, you may choose to leave the nebula with your findings or press on. The encounter ends when you jump away or reach the maximum depth.
